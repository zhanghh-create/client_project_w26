Networking & Connections

Why Networking Matters

Networking is an essential part of career development. Building meaningful connections allows you to learn about industries, roles, and opportunities that may not be visible through job postings alone. For UMSI students, networking can help you gain insights from alumni, peers, and professionals working across a wide range of fields.

Ways to Build Your Network

There are many ways to start networking, even if you are new to the process. Focus on building genuine relationships rather than collecting contacts.

  • Attend career fairs, employer info sessions, and networking events
  • Connect with alumni and professionals on LinkedIn
  • Participate in student organizations and group projects
