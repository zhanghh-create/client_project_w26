Portfolio

What Is a Portfolio?

A portfolio is a curated collection of work that showcases your skills, experiences, and growth over time. Portfolios are commonly used in design, technology, research, and writing fields to demonstrate both process and outcomes.

What to Include in a Strong Portfolio

A strong portfolio highlights selected projects that best represent your abilities. Each project should clearly explain your role, the problem you addressed, and the impact of your work.

  • Project overview and goals
  • Your role and contributions
  • Process, tools, and methods used
  • Final outcomes and reflections
